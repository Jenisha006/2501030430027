        Tomato Dal Tadka

        A hearty, comforting Indian lentil dish tempered with aromatic spices, fresh tomatoes, and garlic. Perfect for a quick weeknight meal.

        25
        Minutes
        2
        Servings
        320
        Calories
        Easy
        Difficulty

        Ingredients

        • Red Lentils1 cup
        • Tomatoes2 medium
        • Onion1 large
        • Garlic4 cloves
        • Cumin seeds1 tsp
        • Turmeric½ tsp
        • Red chili powder1 tsp
        • Oil2 tbsp
        • SaltTo taste
        • Fresh corianderFor garnish

        Instructions

        1. Wash and boil red lentils in 2 cups of water with turmeric and salt until soft (about 15 minutes).

        2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter for 30 seconds.

        3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown, about 5 minutes.

        4. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.

        5. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, and cook until tomatoes soften (4-5 min).

        6. Pour the cooked lentils into the pan and mix well. Simmer for 5 minutes.

        7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
